Internship Objective :
"Internships are crucial and play a vital role in a student's life, leaving a lasting impression on the specialty they aspire to pursue. This experience is essential, as it provides an opportunity to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge gained in a controlled classroom setting and its practical application in real-world scenarios."
The objectives of this internship are to apply theoretical knowledge gained in the classroom to real-world settings, understand the practical challenges of implementing psychological principles, and empower students with the confidence to tackle complex clinical psychological challenges.
Who Can Apply?
Our internship programs are open to:
  • Graduate (BA, BSc) students in psychology and related fields
  • Post-graduate (MA, MSc) students seeking practical experience
  • MPhil candidates looking for specialized training
  • Students pursuing certificate courses in psychology and guidance & counselling
Note : We offer special batches for working professionals (pursuing MA in Clinical / Counseling Psychology from IGNOU) with flexible timings at Humana Wellness in Gurgaon.
Why Choose Us?
  • Experienced Mentors: Learn from professionals with extensive experience in their respective fields.
  • Hands-on Training: Engage in real-world scenarios and case studies.
  • Comprehensive Curriculum: Benefit from a well-rounded program that covers theoretical knowledge and practical skills.
  • Networking Opportunities: Connect with industry experts and peers to build your professional network.
  • Career Development: Gain the confidence and competence to excel in your future career.
  • Exposure in Rehabilitation Setup: Experience working in a real rehabilitation environment to enhance your practical knowledge and skills.
